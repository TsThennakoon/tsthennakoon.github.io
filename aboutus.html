OUR STORY

The Digital Pulz (Pvt.) Ltd. is a campus spin-off that develops cloud-based software solutions for numerous sectors in the healthcare industry. It values innovation, integrity, service excellence and cooperation in all spheres of its work. It established in June 2018 with the intention of providing innovative solutions for uplifting the life standard of patients and the service quality in the health sector.



OUR TEAM

We are a committed team encompassed of Engineers, Developers and Consultants who work closely with the health care, including Doctors, Practitioners, Veterinarians, Lab Technicians, Hospitals, etc., and all about creating the most exquisite, broader cloud-based Hospital Management System (HMS) and Veterinary Practice Management (VPS) on the market. This solution medium ensures that we have all the angles covered when it comes to your Clinics or Labs. We understand how the Health Sector works and has a wealth of industry expertise.



OUR TECHNOLOGY

Digital Pulz is a high quality software solution company, providing rich features and benefits adhering to the latest technology for our customers. As there are no extra licensing fees charged, we keep our pricing choices at a minimum, since our clients are not charged with usage costs. Similarly, we ensure that our customer uses the newest, improved version of the software without any unforeseen upgrade costs down the road, offering unlimited, free updates & lifetime upgrades. In addition, we use Java, PHP, and Spring Framework development languages & frameworks. The software at Digital Pulz contains great technology that strengthens adherence to the latest technology all the time as the eco-system of the open source environment moves forward. In addition, the data of our users is secured equivalent to the protection at the bank level offered by Amazon in their AWS cloud operating in the global hosting environment.
