Modularity

Whole system is divided into simple modules for easy usage of software.

What we are Offering?

OPD

This module is identical to the one included in our product, Mega Pulz. As well as adding the patients to the Out Patient Department Queue (OPD), the initial registrations of patients are done here, but the appointment role is disabled depending on the level ((Private Practicing Doctor / Dispensaries). Nurse / Reception can schedule the doctors and assign the patients on arrival to the particular Diagnostics room. The case file of the patient will then be built allowing the doctor to decide on conducting different tests, medications to the patient. The doctor can also conveniently access the previous medical history of the patient.


Billing

The billing done here is identical to the module in our product, Mega Pulz. But the only difference here is there is only one cashier role, generating service billing, which involves both Diagnostics and Drug billing, without requiring a separate module. Here, too, the cashier will manage advance payment, credit payments and refunds.


Inventory

Inventory module uses to handle stock, suppliers, products, pricing and manufactures. Once pharmacist sends the drug request, storekeeper can create a purchase order and send it to a manager. Then it can continue the PO process and once, supplier deliver products, storekeeper can make good receive note (GRN) and set pricing. Then according to pharmacist requests, stock can dispense. As well as that, if the user has one than one branch, the products in the main stock can transfer. Moreover, this module can handle supplier payments according to GRN. Furthermore, the soon to be expired products in the main stock will notify before two months. According to user requirement, either user can use PO process or skip that and directly start from GRN.
