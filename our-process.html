OUR PROCESS

Here at Digital Pulz, we make sure our processes for all of our products comprises uniformity, simple yet comprehensive that our customers will receive the solutions in a logical and systematic manner. We communicate with our customers throughout the process to ensure the visibility of our work and to maintain the quality of the service provided by us.


1.

Process Mapping

Initially, after extensive discussion with the demand of the client, we come to an understanding of our customer's wants and needs. This awareness lies with visibility between our team and the client and adheres to the new technologies and factors that affect the health sector. Upon knowing the criteria of our client in a very methodical way, we construct a process map and begin to construct the solutions with the starting steps. At the same time, each phase will be well recorded and documented to give our clients an overview of the development process. Our customers profit from this to receive clarity what the processes would look like at the end of production and allow us as a company to provide better customer service.



2.

Mockup Design

The designs will be produced by our development team of UI / UX designers, as a prototype similar to the final product reflecting the process mapping that will give our client an idea of the final project aiming to demonstrate the fonts, icons, color schemes, navigation visuals, images, and content layout. A mockup involves focusing on the overall appearance of software/platform architecture and functionality. These mock ups permit the observation of essential functions of the object where our customer will be given the opportunity to suggest us changes of their liking, to add or eliminate processes, solutions, hardware etc. which is beneficial for the customer eliminating pointless money, time or effort wasted.



3.

Our Technology Stack

After confirmation of the mock up design received by our customers we choose the best, latest and stable platform and programming languages that is Java, PHP, Spring Framework development languages & frameworks. Our technology is an open source which will allow our customer to edit, add, or redistribute the platform of their liking. We make sure our platforms adhere to the latest technology that will give our customer solutions without bugs, glitches or errors. These software are created to be lightweight and to be able to run on any device with a web browser.



4.

The Final Output

The final output that we provide will be tested through a wide range of devices before the delivery to ensure no failures or bugs. We iterate through these steps throughout the life cycle of development process to bring the software that our customer truly deserved. We produce these software to our customer with the minimum pricing options since we do not have to pay and additional license cost. Also, we provide with unlimited, timeless, free updates and upgrades to ensure that our customer provide quality medical services.



5.

Service & Maintenance

We take our service & Maintenance very seriously. We proved 24/7 on call, Remote assistance and maintenance whenever or wherever our customer experience a bug or issue with the software. No bug will see another sunrise when we’re on the job. Before setting out the update, each upgrade & update will be automatically tested and verified to you so that you can rest easy knowing your software is error free and running smoothly to maintain the quality of the clinic.
