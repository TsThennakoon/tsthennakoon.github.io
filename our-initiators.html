Our Initiators

"Digital Pulz was started to provide cost effective software solutions to healthcare service institutions to make their services more efficient and effective. As we are aware, Healthcare services are built around a person in pain. Efficient and effective services in healthcare sector save valuable time of all parties who are struggling in bringing back the normal life to that person. We think our solutions will help to shorten that person’s pain to a minimum so that everyone around that person can be come to normal life quickly and enjoy their lives. We believe that Business is a Pain Reliever which brings contentment to all."

Koliya Pulasinghe PhD, Founder


Koliya Pulasinghe is a Professor in Information Technology, Sri Lanka Institute of Information Technology (SLIIT), Malabe, Sri Lanka. He held numerous positions at SLIIT including Head, Department of Information Technology, Postgraduate Course Coordinator, Dean/Academic Affairs, Dean/Postgraduate Studies & Research, and Dean/Faculty of Computing. He served SLIIT as a member of the Board of Directors from 2007 to 2017. He wants to promote entrepreneurship among his students by example.







"At Digital Pulz we strive to build efficient, reliable, scalable, innovative software solutions that empower hospitals, labs, pharmacies and veterinary hospitals to provide the best service to patients. We use state of the art technology stack to develop our product suite using technologies like Java, Restful Web Services, React Native, PhP and MySQL. We have deployed our solutions in AWS-EC2 cloud, allowing us to scale our solutions on demand."

Nuwan Kodagoda PhD, Co-founder


Dr. Nuwan Kodagoda has over 30 years of experience in the IT sector both in the industry and academia. His main interest is in advocating programming and software engineering best practices for solving real world problems which he does through his industry consultation, research and teaching.
