Other Features

1. Quick Response via SMS

Digital Pulz enables custom variable template messages that allow you to expect direct responses by sending diagnostic reports via SMS. You will be able to submit the link to the diagnostic reports from this feature where the patient can download it and review the report without wasting any time. This will allow you and your patients to save time and effort ensuring the accuracy of sensitive clinical details.


2. Customizable Advance Reporting

At Digital Pulz, we give you the opportunity to receive, in the form of Microsoft Excel, customized reports of your income, inventory management, sales, etc. at your preferred time and import the data into other applications.


3. Reminders

Adding reminders is a simple method with a straightforward approach that allows the option to get more interested with the stock control in the dashboard than conventional report-based reminder systems.


4. Calendar Scheduling

With our calendar planning guide to achieve higher clinic outputs, handle your time more efficiently and set up practical appointments. Simple icons and colors provide visualize different scheduling information such as vaccination, surgery etc.


5. Syndicated/Split Billing

Digital Pulz has a super simple syndicated billing feature that enables each syndicate member to handle syndicates and to break down the billing for their share. Easily credit and re-allocate syndicate invoices as syndicates adjust ownership along with advanced billing and credit management payments.


6. Multi Location Process

Here at Digital Pulz, we have made medical practice and business supervision simpler by understanding the uniqueness of each device and connecting each other.


7. Medical Summaries

Clinical reports can be produced using a predefined template set by Digital Pulz at installation.


8. Create Unlimited Tests And Reports

We have given our clients the ability to perform an infinite number of tests and produce reports in order to retain maximum capacity at the clinic, reducing waiting time, idle time and being competitively beneficial compared to other clinics.


9. Automatic Stock Order Notifications

Digital Pulz lets you monitor inventory and stock, used or on hand, and automatically notify you when you hit a re-order level or below the minimum level! The system provided by us will allow you to determine what inventory to track, including batch and serial numbers.
